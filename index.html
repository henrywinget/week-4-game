Lightsaber Crystal Collector

The Empire needs your help.

As an Imperial Agent, your job is to round up and destory as many lightsaber crystals as you can in compliance with Order 66 and the destruction of the Jedi.

The catch: destroying lightsaber crystals requires massive amounts of energy and the machine can only destroy a specific number of crystals at a time.

The machine will read you a specific number and can only operate if that specific number is met.

Each lightsaber crystal color holds a specific energy value.

Using your vast galactic knowledge, find out the energy value of each crystal and fill up the machine with the exact amount of crystals to meet the energy requirement.

Do not fail your mission: the Emporer is not as forgiving as I am.
